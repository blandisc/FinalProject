Political Sentiment Twitter
Analysis

The purpose of this project is to evaluate the interactions of the Mexican presidential Twitter account before and after the video scandal involving his brother receiving what seems to be illegal campaign financing.

What Happend?

According to Bloomberg:

Two videos surfaced Thursday (August 20th) showing AMLO's brother taking packets of cash from a state political operator in 2015 -- three years before the president was elected. Over the weekend, two recordings that were leaked earlier made their rounds in social media, implicating the leader’s allies in illegal financing antics.

Method

To achieve our objective we scrapped all the text-only responses to the presidential tweets during the last month (August) and classified them, using a Naive Bayes classifier. Finally, we visualized our insights using Tableau

Process

  • Web scrapping using Beautiful Soup 4
  • Pre-Processing using pandas
  • Cleaning the data
  • Training the model
  • Classification
